Indiana Jones

Raiders of the Lost Ark

Lucas conceived Raiders of the Lost Ark in the early 1970s. Seeing to modernize the serial films of the early 20th-century, he developed the idea further with Kaufman, who suggested the Ark as the film's goal. Lucas eventually focused on developing his 1977 space opera Star Wars.

Temple of Doom

The film was released to financial success but the initial reviews were mixed, criticizing its darker elements and violent. However, critical opinion has improved since 1984, citing the film's intensity and imagination.

The Last Crusade

After the mixed reaction to Indiana Jones and the Temple of Doom, Spielberg chose to tone down the dark tone and graphic violent in the next installment. During the five years between The Temple of Doom and The Last Crusade, he and executive producer Lucas reviewed several scripts before accepting Jeffrey Boam's.
